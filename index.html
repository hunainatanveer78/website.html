Online Learning in 2023 - What Is It And How Does It Work?

Thanks to the rapid advancement of technology, online learning is a part of many institutions'
course offerings around the world. Learn more about how it works in this article.








Advances in technology now allow students to study entirely online while still socializing with classmates,
watching lectures and participating in subject-specific discussions.



While some consider online learning to require a greater degree of self-motivation, institutions recognize that educational support
is just as important as tutor feedback, and take great care to ensure that their students receive the same levels of support that they would receive on campus.
